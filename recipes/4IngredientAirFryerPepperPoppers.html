4 Ingredient Air Fryer Pepper Poppers


These 4 ingredient air fryer pepper poppers have all the flavors you love in a popper but without the heat. If you like a tad of heat, feel free to add some pepper Jack cheese to the mixture. These will be the first thing to disappear at your next watch party.

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper,any color
  • 8 ounces cream cheese,softened
  • 1(1 ounce) packet ranch dip mix
  • 2 taplespoons real bacon bits

Directions


Step 1

Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (199 degrees C) according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions.

Step 2

Quarter the bell pepper lengthwise. Remove stem, membrane, and seeds; discard. Cut each quarter in half lengthwise.

Step 3

Mix cream cheese, ranch dip mix, and bacon bits together in a bowl until evenly combined. Using a spoon, divide mixture between pepper slices, about 1 1/2 to 2 teaspoons per slice.

Step 4

Place stuffed bell peppers in the basket of the air fryer. Cook until cheese filling is lightly browned, 8 to 10 minutes. Check after 8 minutes as air fryers vary.
